# Scientific Receipt: Reaction Time Synthesis
**Sources**: Classé et al. (1997), Imamoglu et al. (2000), NCAA Performance Data

## Data Evidence: Simple Reaction Time (Visual)
| Cohort              | Mean RT (ms) | Notes                                |
| :------------------ | :----------- | :----------------------------------- |
| **F1 Drivers**      | 180 - 200    | Race start reaction                  |
| **Elite Sprinters** | 160 - 175    | Block reaction                       |
| **Baseball (Pro)**  | 239.0        | Tracking and swing initiation        |
| **Hockey Goalies**  | 152.0        | Effective window for 100mph slapshot |
| **Table Tennis**    | 120-140      | Choice reaction to spin/vector       |

## Scientific Notes
- Table Tennis and Hockey exhibit the lowest visual latency due to short ball/puck travel distances.
- Choice Reaction Time (deciding how to react) is 50-100% slower than Simple Reaction Time.
- Athletic training reduces SRT by ~15-20% relative to sedentary populations.
