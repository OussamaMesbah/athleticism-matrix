# Scientific Receipt: UFC Performance Institute (2021)
**Source**: "A Cross-Sectional Performance Analysis and Projection of the UFC Athlete, Volume 2"
**Publisher**: UFC Performance Institute, Las Vegas

## Data Evidence: Physiological Benchmarks
Based on testing thousands of elite professional MMA fighters:

| Metric                  | Elite (Top 10%)     | Average UFC Fighter |
| :---------------------- | :------------------ | :------------------ |
| **VO2 Max** (ml/kg/min) | 68.0 - 72.0         | 58.0 - 62.0         |
| **Vertical Jump** (cm)  | 65.0 - 72.0         | 55.0 - 60.0         |
| **Fight Duration**      | 12:35 (Strawweight) | 10:43 (Mean)        |
| **IIR (Injury)**        | N/A                 | ~12.5 (per 100 AE)  |

## Data Evidence: Technical Metrics
- **Strike Rate**: Average 8.5 attempts per minute.
- **Takedown Accuracy**: ~35-45% across divisions.
- **Finish Rate**: 38.2% (KO/TKO), 22.9% (Sub) in Middleweight.

## Implementation in Athleticism Matrix
- MMA is anchored at 150 Durability and 170 Hand-Eye (ms) to reflect the high-velocity, high-consequence nature of the sport.
- Nerve is adjusted based on the "Decision to Commit" and risk of trauma (KO/TKO rates).
