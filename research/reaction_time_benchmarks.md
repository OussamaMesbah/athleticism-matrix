# Research: Neuro-Reactive Benchmarks (Reaction Time)

Reaction time measured in milliseconds (ms). Includes Simple Reaction Time (SRT).

| Category            | Benchmark (ms) | Sport Examples                                       |
| :------------------ | :------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| **World Class SRT** | 150 - 180      | F1 Drivers, 100m sprinters (start), Baseball batters |
| **Elite Pro SRT**   | 180 - 210      | NBA, Soccer, Boxing                                  |
| **General Athlete** | 210 - 250      | Recreational sports                                  |
| **Non-Athlete Avg** | 250 - 300      | General population                                   |

### Noteworthy Metrics:
- **F1 Start Reaction**: Often 170-200ms.
- **Track Start (limit)**: Under 100ms is disqualified (false start).
- **Baseball Pitch Reaction**: A 100mph pitch gives roughly 400ms for total decision, but the physical reaction must trigger in <200ms.
