# Research Receipts: Scientific Evidence Library

This directory contains the "receipts" for the data hardening process. Each file acts as a local archive of the scientific studies, meta-analyses, and professional databases used to anchor the Athleticism Matrix.

## Primary Evidence Files
1.  **[Hootman et al. (2007) - Injury Rates](receipts/hootman_2007_evidence.md)**: Data on 182,000 injuries across 15 sports. used for Durability anchoring.
2.  **[UFC Performance Institute (2021) - MMA Benchmarks](receipts/ufc_pi_2021_evidence.md)**: elite physiological and technical data for combat sports.
3.  **[Haugen et al. (2018) - Human Power Records](receipts/haugen_2018_evidence.md)**: VO2 Max and absolute power records for endurance sports.
4.  **[NBA/NFL Combine Archives](receipts/nba_nfl_combine_synthesis.md)**: Historical vertical jump and explosive power benchmarks.
5.  **[Reaction Time Synthesis](receipts/reaction_time_synthesis_evidence.md)**: Meta-analysis of visual and auditory latency in elite athletes.
